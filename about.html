About Noa

“Values are what lead me in every aspect of my life. First be a person, then a musician.” - Noa

Noa Shnaidman is an Israeli-born vocalist and songwriter based in Boston. Having grown up between Israel and the United States, her music reflects a cross-cultural blend of jazz, R&B, and soul. Her journey began at age nine, when she started taking vocal lessons, and deepened at fifteen, when she commuted daily at 6 a.m. to attend the prestigious Jerusalem Academy of Music and Dance high school. There, she began shaping her identity as a jazz musician and graduated with honors.

She later earned “Excellent Musician” status during her military service, performing across Israel, and continued her studies in the selective jazz institute at Rimon School of Music, where she also performed at national jazz festivals. She is currently studying at Berklee College of Music in Boston, further developing her voice as both a performer and songwriter. “I remember a teacher once telling me, ‘You’re not jazz enough; you’re too soul.’ Later, I realized that was my power. Being a combination of things is what makes me unique.”

Having studied with some of Israel’s leading musicians, Noa blends strong musical training with emotion and instinct, just as her writing process often begins through improvisation. She is currently working on original material with plans to record and release new music in the near future.
